"What Is 'Dark Showering' and Can It Really Help You Sleep Better?"

The impact of our circadian rebellion is becoming impossible to ignore across the health and wellness sector. Healthline recently examined the physiological foundations of the dark shower protocol, noting how it specifically targets light exposure and temperature regulation.
